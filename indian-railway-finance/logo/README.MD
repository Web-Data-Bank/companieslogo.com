## About Indian Railway Finance

Indian Railway Finance Corporation Limited engages in borrowing funds from the financial markets to finance the acquisition/creation of assets that are leased out to the Indian Railways as finance lease in India. It also operates as a non-deposit taking non-banking financial and infrastructure finance company. The company was incorporated in 1986 and is based in New Delhi, India.
