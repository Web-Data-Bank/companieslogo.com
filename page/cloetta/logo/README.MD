## About Cloetta

Cloetta AB (publ) operates as a confectionary company. It manufactures and markets chocolate confectionery products, including pralines, chocolate wafers, dragees, plates, and countlines under the Kexchoklad, Polly, Center, Plopp, Tupla, Royal, Sportlunch, Bridge, Lonka, Sinas, Snippers, and Lonka soft nougat brands.
