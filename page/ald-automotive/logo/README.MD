## About ALD Automotive

ALD S.A. provides service leasing and vehicle fleet management services. Its products and services include full service leasing, fleet management, outsourcing solutions, and sale and lease back; fleet consultancy; tools and services for fleet managers and drivers; and services for maintenance, assistance, availability of a vehicle. The company is also involved in trading used cars and light commercial vehicles; and the retail sale of vehicles. As of December 31, 2021, it operated a fleet of 1.726 million vehicles. The company operates in Western Europe, Central and Eastern Europe, Northern Europe, South America, Africa, and Asia. ALD S.A. was founded in 1946 and is based in Rueil-Malmaison, France.
