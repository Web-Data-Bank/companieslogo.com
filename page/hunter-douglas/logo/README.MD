## About Hunter Douglas

Hunter Douglas N.V. manufactures and sells window coverings and architectural products in Europe, North America, Latin America, Asia, and Australia. It operates through Window Coverings and Architectural Products segments. The Window Coverings segment offers fabric and honeycomb shades, privacy sheers, and roman and roller shades for commercial and residential use.
